Power Pages Plus Minimalist template uses GLightBox. GLightbox is a pure javascript lightbox. It can display images, iframes, inline content and videos with optional autoplay for YouTube, Vimeo and even self hosted videos.

Check out this simple example:

<a href="Myimage.jpg" class="glightbox" data-glightbox="title: Description Left; description: descPosition: left;"> <img src="Myimage.jpg" alt="image"> </a>
